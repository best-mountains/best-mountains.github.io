About the BEST

BEST (Biodiversity along Elevational gradients: Shifts and Transitions)


  • Long-term biodiversity monitoring
  • Multiple contrasting taxa
  • Microclimate monitoring
  • Coordinated distributed network


  • Initialed in 2017
  • Currently, plant surveys in 11 mountains has been done or are ongoing by collaborating with >15 institutes
  • Long-term monitoring (tagging each ind. with > 1cm DBH)
  • Multiple-taxa monitoring: microbes, butterflies, beetles, et al.
  • Microclimate monitoring as one key element
